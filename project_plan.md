# Seyon Fashions — Premium B2B Apparel Manufacturer Website

## 1. Project Description
Seyon Fashions is a vertically integrated apparel manufacturer and exporter from Tiruppur, India, with 35+ years of experience. The website targets international sourcing managers, fashion buyers, and apparel brands from the US, EU, UK, and Australia. Design aesthetic: futuristic, dark-mode-first, luxury B2B (Apple/Tesla/Loro Piana for fashion sourcing). No pricing shown — RFQ (Request for Quote) model only.

## 2. Page Structure
- `/` — Home (cinematic hero, stats, capabilities bento, process flow, world map)
- `/about` — About / Our Story (brand heritage, founder, values, timeline)
- `/capabilities` — Capabilities (6 production stage sections with specs)
- `/sustainability` — Sustainability (certifications, eco metrics, practices)
- `/quality` — Quality & Compliance (certification grid, AQL flowchart)
- `/catalog` — Product Catalog (filterable grid, no pricing, RFQ links)
- `/contact` — Contact & RFQ Form (two-column layout, form, map, WhatsApp)
